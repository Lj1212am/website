About Me

I am a Robotics MSE candidate within the General Robotics, Automation, Sensing, and Preception (GRASP) Lab at the University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia PA. I was previously an undergraduate student researcher within the Institute for Experiential Robotics at Northeastern University, Boston MA. There, I recieved my BS degree of computer engineering and computer science. My research interests include sustainability, swarm robotics, computer vision, path planning, and automation.
My professional interest is robotics research. I would like to explore how autonomous mobile robotics can battle the effects of the climate change.
