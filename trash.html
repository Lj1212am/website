T.R.A.S.H.

Tandem Rover and Aerial Scrap Harvester
(TRASH)

TRASH is an autonomous heterogeneous system to identify and pick up trash. Using Machine Learning to identify images with trash in them (YoLO), Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM) for the Unmanned Air Vehicle (UAV) to build a map for the Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) to move through (OrbSlam 2), Path Planning to travel within the map between each identified piece of trash, and Computer vision (OpenCV) for the UGV to locate itself and move within the map.
